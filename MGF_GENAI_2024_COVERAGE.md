## MGF GenAI 2024 核心原则

新加坡生成式AI治理框架（Proposed Model Governance Framework for Generative AI, 2024）提出了企业部署GenAI时应遵循的核心原则和最佳实践。

### 框架的四大核心维度

1. **风险识别与评估** (Risk Identification and Assessment)
2. **人类监督与问责** (Human Oversight and Accountability)  
3. **技术治理与控制** (Technical Governance and Controls)
4. **透明度与用户教育** (Transparency and User Education)
